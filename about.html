Our Story

Blush & Glow was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional beauty services in a relaxing and welcoming environment. What started as a small boutique salon has grown into a beloved beauty destination known for our expertise and personalized care.

Our founder, Emma Wilson, envisioned a space where clients could escape the stresses of daily life and indulge in self-care. Today, our team of skilled professionals continues this tradition, combining technical expertise with a passion for making clients look and feel their best.

We believe that beauty is about enhancement, not transformation. Our approach focuses on highlighting your natural features and helping you express your unique style.
