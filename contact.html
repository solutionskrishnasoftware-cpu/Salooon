Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book an appointment?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for standard services and 4-6 weeks in advance for bridal packages or major transformations. Weekend appointments tend to fill up quickly, so earlier booking is advised.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost.

Do you use organic products?

Yes, we offer a range of organic and vegan products. Please inform your stylist about any preferences or allergies before your service, and they will be happy to accommodate your needs.

Can I bring my children to my appointment?

While we love children, our salon is designed as a peaceful retreat for adults. For safety reasons and to maintain a relaxing atmosphere, we ask that you make childcare arrangements during your appointment.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! We offer beautifully packaged gift certificates in any denomination. They can be purchased in-salon or over the phone and make perfect gifts for any occasion.
